Our Product Goal and Team Mission:

To utilize our collective knowledge of html, css, and javascript to create a student-friendly, online learning environment that allows members of our cohort to continue to sharpen their individual web dev skills.

A message from our Scrum Master, Juan Batista

While at Generation, I learned to move forward - only forward. I began my day by greeting my colleagues and eagerly listening to Mr. Musa's or Mr. Vasquez's brief recap of what we learned the past day. We then moved on to review the calendar day and review our goals for the day. After our instructor lead lessons, we moved into our asyncronistic assignments. As students, we were fortunate to have two of the finest instructors and staff that helped us meet our goals to find careers and ultimately change our lives and our futures. We worked hard and understand the importance of teamwork and that we are not alone with the help of such effective and dedicated staff. They laid a clear vision for reaching our goals and we are capable of accomplishing anything. At Generation's D.C. cohort we have learned to put our egos behind, discard limiting beliefs and adopt a Growth Mindset. We grow everyday and know that behind every mistake, there is an opportunity to learn something valuable. We can take control of our outcomes because we now have the ability to grow and rather than dwelling on our past failures - we focus on our futures and move towards our success.
